Create a portrait 3:4 educational manga comic page with 4 panels: one top half-page splash and three smaller bottom panels.

[CHARACTER REFERENCE]
小问: young Chinese adult, round glasses, yellow hoodie, laptop stickers, expressive.
蓝袋导师: original round blue robot mentor with white belly pocket, red nose, golden bell collar.
噪音怪: clutter blob made of prompt cards and broken chat bubbles.

[PAGE CONTENT]
Core message: Prompt 像一次性纸飞机，灵活但只能飞一轮。

Panel 1 top half-page: A bright experimental “Prompt 纸飞机实验场”. 蓝袋导师 reveals a gadget labeled “Prompt 纸飞机发射器”. 小问 writes one sentence onto a paper airplane: “帮我总结群聊！” The paper airplane flies toward a glowing AI cloud machine. The AI cloud outputs a neat answer sheet. Narrator box: “Prompt 解决一次对话。” Gadget label in bold: “Prompt 纸飞机”.

Panel 2 bottom-left: The paper airplane completes one loop and dissolves into confetti after delivering the answer. 蓝袋导师 says: “它快、灵活、成本低。”

Panel 3 bottom-middle: 小问 tries another task and has to write the same long instruction again, tired face, pencil shaking. Speech bubble 小问: “但下一次……还得重写？”

Panel 4 bottom-right: 噪音怪 catches discarded paper airplanes and duplicates them into a pile. Speech bubble 噪音怪: “重复劳动，启动！”

Educational manga, clean line art, neutral bright colors, visible motion arcs for paper airplanes, sparkle effects, expressive emotions. No static talking heads; characters are operating gadgets and reacting.
